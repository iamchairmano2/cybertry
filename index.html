Password tips
🔑

Use a passphrase

Combine 4+ random words: mango-cloud-seven-lamp is stronger than a short complex password.

🔒

Never reuse passwords

If one site gets hacked and you reuse passwords, all your accounts are at risk. Use a password manager.

📱

Enable 2FA everywhere

Two-factor authentication means even if someone gets your password, they still can't get in without your phone.

🚫

Avoid personal info

Don't use your name, birthday, school name, or phone number. Attackers try these first.
